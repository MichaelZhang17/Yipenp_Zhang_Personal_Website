My Journey

I was born and raised in Beijing, China. For my middle-school I attended Beijing No.4 Middle School, where I first discovered my love for numbers and logic.

I continued my secondary education at the Experimental High School Affiliated to Beijing Normal University, diving deeper into advanced math courses and participating in science fairs.

Halfway through, I moved abroad to attend Grand Rapids Christian High School in Michigan, USA. Immersed in a new curriculum, I excelled in calculus and statistics while adapting to a different culture.

Mathematics had always been my strongest subject—solving puzzles as a child or competing in math contests. When I arrived at university, I realized that “math” branched into countless fields. A Data Science course opened my eyes to how statistical modeling, machine learning, and visualization can turn raw data into compelling stories and actionable insights. I immediately knew this was the path for me.
